Welcome To Computer Engineering

Hello! Welcome to the BS Computer Engineering program of the Ateneo de Davao University! Thank you for choosing this course as the stepping stone to your future career. In this program, your skills will be honed and armed to tackle real-world problems with fun and creative automated solutions. Expect lots of fun lessons and activities especially in the fields of programming and mathematics!

The following are the Frequently Asked Questions.

1. How to access my Student Information System?

To access your Student Information System, your username should be composed of the first letter of
your name followed by your middle initial and your last name.

Example:
Name: Clara Gilmore P. Bautista
username: cgpbautsita

For your password, type AdDU then followed by your student ID

Example:
password: AdDU123456789090

2. How do I access my University Email?

The email address for you University Email is similar with the username in your SIS account but you need to append @addu.edu.ph to your username.

Example:
SIS username: cgpbautsita
Email Address: cgpbautsita@addu.edu.ph

And your password is the same with your SIS account.
